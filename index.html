Welcome to Inter

A thinking tool that builds your mindmap as you type — connecting ideas, finding patterns, and surfacing what's missing.

  • 1Type any word or idea below and press Enter. Add as many as you like, one at a time.
  • 2The map builds and connects itself automatically as your ideas grow.
  • 3Use ✦ Suggest gaps to find missing ideas, and ✳ Tidy map to clean up layout and remove duplicates.
  • 4Click any node to rename it, set it as the core concept, or delete it. Drag nodes to reposition them freely.
  • 5Hit Export PNG when done — saves a clean, tight image of your map.
